About Me

Hey there! I'm Anthony, and I'm a software developer by trade. I'm currently building out my own start-up, Self Logistics, while also providing technical advisory and the occasional investment to others.

I previously worked at Shopify as a developer and manager, contributing to products such as Shopify Shipping, Shopify Fulfillment Network, local pickup, and the Cart API. Prior to Shopify I was at Queen's University studying Computer Engineering and Economics.

I knew I wanted to get involved with technology from a young age. One of my cousins stayed with my family when studying computer science in university and I wanted to do what he could do. I've done full stack web and mobile projects for school, work, and for fun. Aside from coding, I'm most proud of a couple of things I started and used to run: Queen's Conference on Business and Technology (a club that continues to run), and PromoBands (which gave me my true intro to e-commerce, well before joining Shopify!).

In my spare time I enjoy playing music (guitar and piano), cooking, taking pictures, golfing, and watching football and hockey.
